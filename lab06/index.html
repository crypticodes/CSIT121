Wounded Warrior Project - Site Review

What is the URL of the website?

The URL can be found here: Wounded Warrior Project

What is the name of the website?

This site is the Wounded Warrior Project

Who is the site's target audience?

This site targets veterans/service members who incurred a physical or mental injury, illness or wound while serving on or after September 11, 2001. This also extends to families and carigivers of these veterans, and active duty or transitioning service members.

How is the site organized?

The page follows a hierarchical organization, as the user must navigate categories and subcategories to find content. There is no 1 linear path to content. There are sub pages that link to different topics.

Which CRAP Design Principle does the use?

The site uses all 4 principles of CRAP:
Contrast - The site uses bold colors and larger font for wording like "Donate"
Repetition - There is consistent use of the colors red, white, and blue along the page
Alignment - Headings and buttons are formatted neatly
Proximity - Images and descriptions are grouped together & white space is used to separate content/topics
